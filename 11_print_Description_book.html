Books

“The Principles” is the latest book by Ray Dalio — founder of Bridgewater, the world’s

largest hedge fund. In this rather hefty tome of a book, Dalio offers an insight into

the principles which he’s applied throughout his life and work, and his underlying reflections.

He kicks off the book by explaining that “Good principles are effective ways of dealing with reality” and that “To learn my own, I spend a lot of time reflecting.”
